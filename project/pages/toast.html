Notes

  • His name was originally Charlie
  • My older brother rescued him in South Carolina Pre-Covid, and then left him with us in Connecticut during Covid.
  • People think he's fat, but he is just very long and often scrunched.
  • He's a friendly dude, he likes to be around people.
  • He almost had to be amputated because of something in South Carolina, but he miraculously recovered completely.
  • There really is something special about Toast.
  • Often my Dad absentmindedly lets him out, but he just hangs around the door and waits to come back inside.
