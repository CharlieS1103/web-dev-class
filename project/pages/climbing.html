Why I climb

I am a very avid climber during the summer, though I often stop during the school year.

I really like the level of stability and strength needed for rock climbing, and I got really into training for it. The part I really love is how many cool "tricks" it unlocks. Most of which sort of look like the stuff you'd see in a New York Subway, swinging from the bars and such.
